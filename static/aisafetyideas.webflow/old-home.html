AI Safety Prizes

The AI Safety Prizes program tracks progress on the most important issues for AI Safety today. Besides tracking the main development and research vectors, AI Satefy Prizes also allows donators to add a prize for an issue they would like solved with that prize before a deadline. The AI Safety Prizes program is maintained by the A*PART Research team. If you would like to create and/or fund a prizes, click the button "Create a prize" and we will review it.
